Proximal Convoluted Tubule (PCT)

The PCT is responsible for the reabsorption of water, ions, and nutrients from the filtrate.

Henle's Loop

Distal Convoluted Tubule (DCT)

The DCT is involved in the selective reabsorption of ions and the regulation of pH.

Collecting Duct

The collecting duct is responsible for the final concentration of urine and the reabsorption of water.

Renal Calculi

Renal calculi, or kidney stones, are hard deposits made of minerals and salts that form inside the kidneys.

Glomerulonephritis

Glomerulonephritis is an inflammation of the kidney's filtering units, which can lead to kidney damage.
